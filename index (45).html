Eligibility

General Requirements

Requirements vary by employer. Always confirm specifics directly with each company before applying.

What You Typically Need

  • Valid US driver's license for the role
  • Minimum age of 18 (21+ for some roles)
  • Legal work authorization in the US
  • Pass a background and driving record check
  • Reliable vehicle in good condition (gig roles)
  • Valid auto insurance meeting employer minimums

What May Affect Eligibility

  • Suspended or revoked license
  • Multiple serious traffic violations recently
  • DUI or DWI convictions (varies by employer)
  • Vehicle not meeting safety requirements
  • Failed drug screening (where required)
  • No valid US work authorization

For CDL / Commercial Roles

  • Valid CDL license required
  • Age 21+ for interstate driving (most cases)
  • DOT medical certification may be needed
  • Experience varies by carrier and route
  • Endorsements (HazMat, Tanker) for some loads
  • Each carrier sets its own standards
FAQ

Common Questions

Honest answers — no exaggeration, no false claims.

Is signing up really free?
Yes. No payment is required to register or browse listings. We earn a referral fee from partner platforms — not from you.
Does signing up guarantee a job?
No. Signing up gives you access to listings. Whether you receive an offer depends entirely on the employer and your application.
Will an interview be scheduled for me?
No. We do not schedule interviews. Any interview is arranged directly between you and the employer through their own process.
Are jobs available in my area right now?
We cannot confirm which positions are currently open. Availability changes frequently. After signing up, you can filter listings by your location.
Do I need my own vehicle?
For gig roles like rideshare and food delivery, yes. For employed positions like trucking or package delivery, the employer usually provides the vehicle.
Will there be a background check?
Most driving employers run a background check including your driving record. Specific requirements vary — check each employer's policies before applying.
Is my information protected?
We handle your data responsibly and do not share it without your knowledge. Also review the privacy policy of any employer platform you use separately.
