Eligibility

General Requirements

Requirements differ by employer and job type. The points below reflect common criteria across most driving roles in the US. Always verify specifics directly with each employer before applying.

🟣 What You Typically Need

  • A valid US driver's license appropriate for the role
  • Minimum age of 18 (some roles require 21 or older)
  • Legal authorization to work in the United States
  • Ability to pass a background and driving record check
  • Reliable vehicle in good condition (for gig-based roles)
  • Valid auto insurance meeting the employer's minimum requirements

🩷 What May Affect Eligibility

  • A suspended or revoked license will disqualify most applications
  • Multiple serious traffic violations in recent years
  • DUI or DWI convictions (policies vary by employer)
  • Vehicle that does not meet safety or age requirements
  • Inability to pass a required drug screening where applicable
  • No valid work authorization in the US

🩵 For Commercial / CDL Roles

  • A valid Commercial Driver's License (CDL) is required
  • Minimum age of 21 for interstate driving in most cases
  • DOT medical certification may be required
  • Experience requirements vary by carrier and route type
  • Endorsements (HazMat, Tanker, etc.) may be needed for specific loads
  • Each company has its own hiring standards — check directly
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, honest answers to the most common questions we receive.

Is this completely free to sign up?
Yes, completely free. You will not be asked for any payment to register or browse job opportunities. We receive a referral fee from partner platforms when you successfully connect — not from you.
Does signing up guarantee me a job?
No. Signing up gives you access to job listings and hiring platforms. Whether you receive a job offer depends entirely on the employer — their current needs, your application, and whether you meet their requirements.
Will an interview be arranged for me automatically?
No. We do not schedule interviews on your behalf. If an employer requires an interview, that will be arranged directly between you and them as part of their own hiring process.
Are jobs currently available in my area?
We cannot confirm which specific positions are open at any given moment, as availability changes frequently. After signing up, you can explore current listings filtered by your location to see what's relevant to you.
What kind of driver's license do I need?
It depends on the role. Standard delivery and rideshare jobs typically require only a regular Class D license. Trucking and commercial roles require a CDL. Medical transport and specialty roles may have additional licensing requirements. Each listing will specify what's needed.
Do I need my own vehicle?
For gig-based roles such as rideshare and food delivery, yes — a personal vehicle is typically required. For employed positions like package delivery or trucking, employers often provide the vehicle. It varies by role and employer.
Will there be a background check?
Most driving employers conduct a background check, which typically includes your driving record and may include a criminal history review. Requirements vary by company and role. We recommend reviewing each employer's policies before you apply.
I have a past violation. Can I still apply?
Possibly. Minor or older infractions may not disqualify you from many roles. More serious offenses — like a DUI — are more likely to affect eligibility for certain positions. Each employer makes their own determination. It is always worth applying and allowing them to review your record directly.
Is my personal information protected?
We handle your information responsibly and do not share it without your knowledge. We also encourage you to read the privacy policy of any employer platform you use, as they have their own separate data practices.
What if a position I find is no longer available?
Job availability changes regularly. If a position you're interested in is no longer open, we encourage you to explore other listings on the platform. New opportunities are added frequently across different locations and categories.
